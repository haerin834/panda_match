About Panda Match

Welcome to Panda Match!

Panda Match is a fun and engaging web-based match-3 puzzle game where players match three identical blocks to clear them from the board.

How to Play

  1. Select three identical blocks to clear them from the board.
  2. Aim to complete each level within the limited number of moves.
  3. Use special items like reshuffle, withdraw, and add buffer to help you progress.
  4. Earn points and compete with other players on the leaderboards.

Special Items

  • Reshuffle: Randomly reorganize all tiles on the board.
  • Withdraw: Cancel your current selection of tiles.
  • Add Buffer: Add new tiles to the board.

Leaderboards

Challenge yourself and other players by competing on our daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards!
